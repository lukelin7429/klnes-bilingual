Sustainability · 永續教育

A Visit About
the Earth's Future

國際婦女會參訪 · 永續環境在地實踐
April 28, 2026 ·2026 年 4 月 28 日 ·International Women's Association · 國際婦女會

The International Women's Association came to see how a small school turns the word “sustainability” into something children do every single day.

國際婦女會來訪，看一所小學如何把「永續」這個詞，變成孩子每天都在做的事。

Guests From Far Away 遠道而來的貴賓

On April 28, our school welcomed a meaningful visit from the International Women's Association. Principal Wang personally led a tour of the campus and gave a special talk, sharing with the guests the school's efforts and results in sustainable environmental education.

4 月 28 日，學校迎來了一場充滿意義的交流盛會——國際婦女會蒞臨參訪。由勝忠校長親自帶領校園導覽，並進行專題講座，向遠道而來的貴賓們分享學校在永續環境教育上的努力與成果。

From the way the campus spaces are planned, to food-and-farming education woven into lessons, to the real actions the children take part in — step by step, we make “sustainability” not just an idea, but a learning journey that truly happens in daily life. Every corner, every experience, is a starting point for a child to understand the land and treasure its resources.

從校園空間的規劃，到課程中的食農教育、環境議題融入教學，再到孩子們親身參與的實踐行動，我們一步一腳印，讓「永續」不只是理念，而是真正發生在日常生活中的學習歷程。每一處角落、每一次體驗，都是孩子理解土地、珍惜資源的重要起點。

“Sustainability is not a word on a poster. It is something a child does today.”

永續不是海報上的一個詞，而是一個孩子今天就在做的事。

Through cross-cultural dialogue and sharing, the visit let the world see KangLang's character — and gave us fresh inspiration in return. Our thanks to the International Women's Association. In the future, we will keep our roots in the local and our eyes on the world, leading our children toward a wider horizon.

在交流的過程中，透過跨文化的對話與分享，不僅讓世界看見槺榔國小的教育特色，也讓我們獲得更多啟發與感動。感謝國際婦女會的蒞臨指導。未來，我們也將持續深耕在地、連結國際，帶領孩子走向更寬廣的世界，實踐永續的教育願景。
