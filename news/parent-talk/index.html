Learn Well

親職教育講座 · 有效學習的攻略
March 10, 2026 ·2026 年 3 月 10 日 ·Parent Education Talk · 班親會專題講座

A talk for parents on how children really learn — from the 1200-word English handbook, recorded word by word, to a weekly vocabulary check.

一場給家長的講座，談孩子如何真正學會——從逐字錄音的英語 1200 字手冊，到每週的單字檢測。

A Talk for Parents 給家長的一場講座

At this year's parent meeting, Director Mei-lan invited the principal to give a talk for families on effective learning strategies for their children — offering all kinds of learning resources, and sharing how to prepare for the entrance exams at each stage of school.

今年的班親會，美蘭主任特別安排校長進行了一場講座，針對學校家長們分享孩子有效學習的攻略，除了提供各式學習資源外，也分享了現今各階段升學考試的準備方式。

Language learning has its golden window, the talk explained, and catching it makes learning far more effective. That is why KangLang is putting real people and real resources into international education and bilingual teaching — so that, even in elementary school, our students meet a rich, generous flow of language.

眾所周知，語言學習有其黃金時期，把握住就能事半功倍。因此講座中特別提到，本校正在推動國際教育與雙語教學，特別是英語教學的部分導入了許多人力物力資源，就是要讓學生在國小階段就能有更大量的語言學習刺激與更好的學習環境。

“Effective learning is learning you can use.”

有效的學習，是你用得出來的學習。

A Word Book, Recorded Word by Word 一本逐字錄音的單字手冊

It began with the 1200-word English resource. The school's foreign teacher and English team together wrote a 1200-word study handbook, and for every single word they recorded the audio and wrote an example sentence. With the parents' association's help, every student now holds a copy — and with a weekly vocabulary check, children learn step by step, getting better and better.

從英語 1200 字的學習資源講起，我們集合了學校的外師與英語教學團隊，共同編撰了英語 1200 字學習手冊，還針對每個字進行錄音與例句撰寫，結合家長會的資源讓每個學生人手一本，加上每週的英語單字檢測，讓孩子分段學習，循序漸進，越學越好。

Section leader Qi-qin even used his information-technology skills to build a 1200-word website, so parents can sit beside their children and learn at home, with no gaps in the journey. When learning is both fun and effective, children begin to learn on their own — and lifelong learning stops being a dream and becomes a goal within reach.

另外，騏駸組長還發揮資訊專長，幫我們建構了一個英語 1200 字的網站，方便家長在家陪孩子自主學習，讓學習沒有斷層、更有樂趣。當學習有趣又有效，孩子有了成就感，就會主動學習，培養能終身學習的學習者便不是夢想，而是指日可待的目標。
