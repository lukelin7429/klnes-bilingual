英文繪本故事班正式開跑
March 4, 2026 ·2026 年 3 月 4 日 ·English Picture-Book Storytime · 英文繪本故事班

Listen first, read later. A new storytime class gives younger children “English ears” through funny, magical, and exciting picture books.

先聽說、後讀寫。一個新的繪本故事班，用幽默、魔幻、刺激的故事，幫中低年級的孩子養出「英文耳朵」。

A New Class Begins 一個新班級開跑

KangLang's English picture-book storytime is officially under way! Our special thanks to the Ya-Xiu Charity Foundation for its generous funding, which supports the school in opening this class and building an even better language-learning environment for our children.

槺榔國小英文繪本故事班正式開跑囉！特別感謝財團法人雅秀社會福利慈善基金會慷慨挹注經費，支持本校開設英文繪本故事班，為孩子們打造更優質的語言學習環境。

The key to language learning is meeting it early and moving step by step, following the principle of “listen and speak first, read and write later.” So this class is mainly for our younger and middle-grade students, and uses rich, lively English picture books as its teaching material.

語言學習的關鍵在於及早接觸與循序漸進，並遵循「先聽說、後讀寫」的原則。因此，本課程以本校中、低年級學生為主要對象，選用內容豐富、生動有趣的英文繪本作為教學媒材。

“First the ear, then the page. First the story, then the word.”

先有耳朵，才有書頁；先有故事，才有單字。

Growing English Ears 養出英文耳朵

Through stories that are magical, funny, silly, or thrilling, we guide children to slip naturally into an English world. In repeated listening and interaction, they build a feel for the language and train their “English ears.”

透過故事中魔幻、幽默、無厘頭或緊張刺激的情節，引導孩子自然融入英語情境，在反覆聆聽與互動中培養語感，訓練「英文耳朵」。

We hope that, in a relaxed and happy story atmosphere, children will come close to English, like English, and quietly build confidence and interest — and even start picking up English books on their own, so that reading becomes a window onto the world and a firm foundation for the language learning to come.

我們期盼透過輕鬆愉快的故事氛圍，讓孩子願意親近英文、喜歡英文，並在潛移默化中建立自信與興趣，更希望孩子能因此養成主動翻閱英文書籍的習慣，讓閱讀成為探索世界的一扇窗，為未來的語言學習奠定穩固基礎。

Learn the Words from This Story

十個關鍵字 · 點 🔊 聽單字，再點小喇叭聽整句
1
story(n.)

The teacher reads us a new story.
老師念一個新故事給我們聽。

A picture book has words and pictures.
繪本裡有文字也有圖畫。

聽

我們先聽故事，再讀它。
4

很快我就能自己讀這本書了。

6

每天閱讀是個好習慣。
2Try the Conversation
對話練習 · 兩個人一組，A 和 B 輪流唸
🎬 Scene · After storytime, two younger students chat about the book.
情境：故事時間結束後，兩位低年級同學聊起那本書。
A
Did you like the story today?
你喜歡今天的故事嗎？
B
Yes! It was so funny. The cat could fly!
喜歡！好好笑，那隻貓會飛！
A
I know! I want to read it again.
對啊！我想再讀一次。
B
Me too. I understand more English now.
我也是。我現在聽懂更多英文了。
A
Let's borrow an English book together.
我們一起借一本英文書吧。
B
Good idea! Reading is fun.
好主意！閱讀很好玩。
