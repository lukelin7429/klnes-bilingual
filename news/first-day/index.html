The First Morning
of a New Term

開學日 · 校長的三個叮嚀
February 24, 2026 ·2026 年 2 月 24 日 ·First Day of the Spring Term · 開學始業式

Three reminders, two notes, and one wish — the principal's welcome on the first day back, and a quiet idea: real learning is what you can use.

三個叮嚀、兩個提醒、一個祝福——校長在開學第一天的歡迎詞，還有一個安靜的想法：真正的學習，是你用得出來的學習。

Good Morning, Everyone 各位同學，早安

Good morning, dear students! A new term has begun. Seeing everyone so full of energy, and hearing you greet me so warmly, makes the principal very happy. Today, on the first morning back, I'd like to give you three reminders, two notes, and one wish, to open this learning journey together.

各位親愛的同學，大家早安！新的學期開始了，看到大家精神飽滿、熱情地與校長打招呼問好，校長心裡非常開心。今天是新學期開學的始業式，校長想用三個叮嚀、兩個提醒，以及一個祝福，陪大家一起開啟新的學習旅程。

The three reminders: come to school on time and start class on time; stay safe and follow the rules; and finish the work that is yours to do. The two notes: keep our campus friendly and be kind to your classmates; and build good, healthy habits for a healthy life.

三個叮嚀：第一，準時到校、準時上課；第二，注意安全、遵守規定；第三，完成分內工作。兩個提醒：第一，友善校園、友愛同學；第二，養成良好衛生習慣、健康生活。

“Reading is not a contest of who sits longest. It is learning you can truly use.”

讀書不是比誰坐得久，而是學會、記住、用得出來。

One Wish 一個祝福

And finally, one wish: may your learning be effective, and your effort truly count. Reading is not a contest of who can sit the longest. It is about who can really understand, remember, and use what they learn in real life — learning to solve problems for the world and the future ahead.

最後，校長要送給大家一個祝福：學習有效，有效學習。讀書不是比誰坐得久，而是比誰學得會、記得住、在生活當中可以應用出來，學會解決問題，應用於未來的社會與環境。

Please believe this: as long as you are willing to try, your teachers will walk with you, the principal will support you, and the school will help you. Treasure each day — live earnestly, learn with heart, treat others kindly, and look after yourself. I wish you all peace, joy, and a little progress, every single day.

請相信：只要你肯努力，老師會陪你、校長會支持你、學校會幫助你。把握每一天，認真生活、用心學習、善待他人、照顧自己。祝福大家天天平安、天天快樂、天天進步！
