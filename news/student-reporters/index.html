Student Achievement · 學生榮耀

Our Student Reporters
on National TV

小記者調查隊影片上架
January 27, 2026 ·2026 年 1 月 27 日 ·Culture Qingshui · Mandarin Daily News Channel · 文化清水 · 國語日報

Four KangLang student reporters interviewed a fishing port and a military village — and their “Culture Qingshui” videos are now on a national channel.

四位槺榔小記者採訪了漁港與眷村，他們的「文化清水」影片，現在登上了國語日報的探索頻道。

Happy Winter Holiday! 寒假快樂！

Wonderful news to start the winter break! In the summer of 2025, four of KangLang's excellent student reporters — Zhang Shuo-ying, Chen You-an, Xu Yi-xin, and Xie Qiong-ru — took part in interviews for the “Culture Qingshui” video series. Those videos have now been published on the Mandarin Daily News Explore channel on YouTube!

寒假快樂！本校優秀的小記者張碩穎、陳宥安、許以欣、謝瓊汝，在 2025 年參與採訪的「文化清水」影片，已經刊登在 YouTube 國語日報探索頻道了！

The series, made by the 2025 Summer Student-Reporter Investigation Team, explored how a fishing port and a military-dependants' village have grown and thrived together — one episode on the Qingshui military village, another on Wuqi Fishing Port.

這套影片由 2025 暑假小記者調查隊製作，主題「漁港與眷村共榮」——一集是清水眷村篇，一集是梧棲漁港篇。

“Give a child a microphone, and they will show you their hometown.”

給孩子一支麥克風，他們會帶你認識自己的家鄉。

Warm and Professional 熱情又專業

Whether in a printed newspaper or in a moving video, our young reporters were warm and professional all the way through. Don't wait — head to the comments and watch our students' wonderful interviews for yourself!

不論是靜態報紙，或動態影片，小記者們的表現都是十分熱情又專業！話不多說，快移步到留言處，觀賞同學們的精彩採訪吧！
