六年乙班認識了以色列的食物、語言與名人，也用中音笛吹奏聖誕歌曲回應，把喜悅與和平送到地球的另一端。

A Lesson in Israeli Culture 一堂以色列文化課

Class 602 had an online exchange with Ofek–Giat Zeev School in Israel. After warming up with self-introductions at our first meeting, the Israeli students gave us a rich tour of their country — its food, its schools, its language and capital city, its inventions, and its famous people and singers. It felt like a whole lesson in Israeli culture.

六年乙班與以色列 Ofek–Giat Zeev 學校進行了線上交流。第一次見面、以自我介紹暖身之後，以色列的小朋友詳細地向我們介紹了他們的食物、學校、語言與首都、發明，還有名人與歌手，讓我們上了豐富的一課以色列文化。

Joy and Peace, on the Recorder 用直笛吹出喜悅與和平

In return, we performed something we had learned in music class — a Christmas song on the alto recorder.

我們則向以色列的小朋友表演了在音樂課所學——用中音笛吹奏一首聖誕歌曲。

In the warm, festive spirit of Christmas, we sent happiness, love, and peace all the way to the other side of the Earth.

在聖誕歡樂的節慶氣氛裡，我們把快樂、喜悅、愛與和平，傳送到地球的另一端。

“We sent joy, love, and peace across the Earth.”

我們把喜悅、愛與和平，傳送到地球的另一端。
