Friends Across the Sea: Indonesia

國際教育・第一課：海的那邊，印尼好朋友

Indonesia is a big, friendly country with many islands. Let's say hello, learn beautiful things from our friends, and try a fun quiz!
印尼是一個有很多島嶼、很友善的國家。我們一起打招呼、學習印尼的美好事物，再玩個小測驗吧！

II · Map

Where Is Indonesia? 🗺️

印尼在哪裡？就在台灣的南邊海上

Indonesia is south of Taiwan, across the warm sea. It has more than 17,000 islands — that is a lot of islands! Many friends, many smiles. 印尼在台灣的南邊，越過溫暖的大海。它有超過 1 萬 7 千個島嶼，好多好多島！很多朋友，很多笑容。

IV · Learn

Beautiful Things to Learn from Indonesia

印尼值得我們學習的美好事物 · 點 🔊 聽英文句子
🤝
Gotong Royong
大家一起幫忙
In Indonesia, people work together to help each other. "Many hands make light work."在印尼，大家一起合作、互相幫忙。人多力量大！
We help each other.
🌏
Unity in Diversity
不同，卻是一家人
Many islands, many people, one happy country. We are all different, and that is good!很多島、很多人，卻是一個快樂的國家。我們都不一樣，這樣很好！
One big family.
🎨
Batik Art
蠟染布的藝術
Batik is beautiful cloth with patterns. Making it takes time and patience.巴蒂克是有花紋的漂亮布。做它需要時間和耐心。
Art takes patience.
🎍
Angklung Music
竹子做的樂器
Angklung is made of bamboo. Everyone plays one note, so we must play as a team.昂格隆是竹子做的。每個人負責一個音，所以要團隊合作。
Play as a team.
🦎
Caring for Nature
愛護大自然
Indonesia has rainforests, orangutans, and the big Komodo dragon. We should keep them safe.印尼有雨林、紅毛猩猩，還有大科莫多龍。我們要保護牠們。
Protect nature.
🍢
Sharing Food
分享食物
Friends share yummy food like satay and fried rice (nasi goreng). Sharing is caring!朋友會分享好吃的食物，像沙嗲和炒飯。分享就是關心！
Sharing is caring.

VI · Talk

Meeting a Friend from Indonesia

小對話 · 跟著開口說，或按 ▶ 整段播放
🗣️
Say Hello to Budi
A = You (Taiwan) · B = Budi (Indonesia)
A
Halo! My name is Ming. I am from Taiwan.
哈囉！我叫小明，我來自台灣。
B
Halo, Ming! My name is Budi. Nice to meet you!
哈囉，小明！我叫 Budi，很高興認識你！
A
Wow, Indonesia has many islands! Can you teach me a word?
哇，印尼有好多島！可以教我一個字嗎？
B
Yes! We say "terima kasih." It means thank you.
好啊！我們說 terima kasih，意思是謝謝。
A
Terima kasih, Budi! Let's be friends.
謝謝你，Budi！我們做朋友吧。
B
Sama-sama! Yes, we are friends now. Teman!
不客氣！對，我們現在是朋友了。Teman（朋友）！
