Unboxing a Gift
From Türkiye

開箱！土耳其的文化禮物
January 13, 2025 ·2025 年 1 月 13 日 ·Grade 5 · 五年級 ·İstanbul Ticaret Odası BİLSEM, Türkiye · 土耳其

After the end-of-term ceremony came the moment our fifth graders had waited for — opening a culture box from Türkiye, full of handwritten cards, bookmarks, a woven backpack, and the famous blue evil-eye charm.

結業式之後，五年級孩子引頸期盼的時刻到了——打開來自土耳其的文化箱，裡頭有手寫卡片、書籤、編織背包，還有著名的藍眼睛護身符。

The Long-Awaited Unboxing 期待已久的開箱

Our fifth graders received a culture box from İstanbul Ticaret Odası BİLSEM in Türkiye! The long-awaited unboxing took place right after our end-of-semester ceremony.

五年級的孩子收到了土耳其 İstanbul Ticaret Odası BİLSEM 寄來的文化箱！這場引頸期盼的開箱活動，就在學期結業式之後進行。

Inside the box were handwritten cards, İstanbul-style bookmarks, an ethnic-patterned backpack, a charming folding mirror, and Türkiye's famous blue “evil eye” charm.

土耳其寄來的箱子裡，不只裝著一張張手寫卡片，還有伊斯坦堡風的書籤、富有民族風的背包、精巧的摺疊鏡，以及土耳其著名的藍色「邪眼」護身符。

Words That Became Friendship 化作友誼的文字

Every handwritten card was filled with warm words of friendship. As we read them, those words turned into a quiet, fluttering joy in our hearts.

每一張手寫卡片裡，滿滿的文字，都是滿滿的友誼。讀著讀著，那些字句化作我們心田裡翩翩飛舞的感動。

“Each card was filled with words of friendship.”

每一張卡片，都寫滿了友誼。
