International Exchange · A Course at KangLang

Making Friends with Indonesia

國際交流課程・和印尼做朋友

Our exchange course takes us across the sea to meet a new friend — Indonesia. We learn it lesson by lesson: say hello, discover beautiful things, and make a friend.
這門交流課程帶我們越過海洋、認識新朋友——印尼。我們一課一課慢慢學：打招呼、認識美好的事物、交朋友。

Each lesson has a handout on the left and a mini quiz on the right, with a 🔊 speaker on every word so you can hear how it sounds.每一課左邊是講義、右邊是小考，每個字旁邊都有 🔊 喇叭，點一下就能聽發音。
