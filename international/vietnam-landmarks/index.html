Traveling Between
Taiwan and Vietnam

在台灣與越南之間旅行
April 28, 2026 ·2026 年 4 月 28 日 ·Grade 5 · 五年級 ·Vinschool, Vietnam · 越南

Without boarding a single plane, our fifth graders “visited” both countries through a screen — sharing Taiwan's most beautiful places and discovering Vietnam's famous sights in return.

沒搭上任何一班飛機，五年級的孩子透過螢幕「走訪」了兩個國家——分享台灣最美的地方，也認識越南的著名景點。

KangLang × Vinschool · a tour of Taiwan and Vietnam, screen to screen
槺榔國小 × 越南 Vinschool・隔著螢幕，一場台越之旅

Showing Off the Island We Love 介紹我們深愛的島嶼

This was our fifth graders' second international exchange, and after their first time they were noticeably calmer and more confident. Connecting online with Vinschool in Vietnam, the classroom suddenly reached across a border.

這是五年級的第二次國際交流。有了第一次的經驗，大家明顯多了一份從容與自信。與越南 Vinschool 線上連線，教室瞬間跨越了國界。

In groups, our students introduced Taiwan's famous spots — sunny Kenting, picture-perfect Sun Moon Lake, the old streets of Lukang, and the mountain town of Jiufen. One group proudly introduced our own hometown, Qingshui.

我們分組介紹台灣的特色景點——陽光熱情的墾丁、風景如畫的日月潭、古色古香的鹿港、山城風情的九份，還有一組驕傲地介紹我們的家鄉清水。

A Window Onto Vietnam 望向越南的一扇窗

In return, the Vietnamese students introduced their own famous sights: the spectacular Dragon Bridge, the dreamy Ba Na Hills, the much-photographed Golden Bridge, and the natural beauty of the Marble Mountains.

越南的小朋友也介紹了他們的著名景點：壯觀的龍橋（Dragon Bridge）、夢幻的巴拿山（Ba Na Hills）、吸引無數遊客的金橋（Golden Bridge），以及自然景觀豐富的五行山（Marble Mountains）。

Through each other's sharing, the children opened a window onto a different country. Even without leaving the classroom, they took a memorable trip across two lands.

透過彼此的分享，孩子們打開了一扇望向不同國家的窗。即使沒離開教室，也走了一場難忘的雙國之旅。

“We traveled to two countries without leaving the classroom.”

我們沒離開教室，就旅行了兩個國家。
